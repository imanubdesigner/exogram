# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

Exogram is an open-source project that values respectful, constructive collaboration. Everyone who participates in this project — through issues, pull requests, discussions, or any other channel — is expected to follow this code of conduct.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions
- Give constructive, specific feedback
- Accept criticism gracefully and focus on the work, not the person
- Prioritize the health and safety of the community

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or content (related to gender, sexual orientation, disability, ethnicity, religion, or other protected characteristics)
- Publishing others' private information without explicit consent
- Any behavior that could be considered harmful in a professional context

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces: GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other communication channel related to Exogram.

## Reporting

If you witness or experience behavior that violates this code of conduct, open a [GitHub Discussion](https://github.com/matzalazar/exogram/discussions) or reach out to the maintainers directly through a private Issue.

Reports will be treated confidentially. We commit to responding within 5 business days.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in a warning, temporary suspension, or permanent ban from the project, depending on the severity and history of the behavior.
