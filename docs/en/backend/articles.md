# App: articles

Editorial articles for the project: philosophy, manifesto, mission.

---

## Purpose

Static content (but manageable from the admin) that explains the project to readers.
Unlike the rest of the apps, the content is not generated by users: it is published by the team.

---

## Models

### `Article`

- `title`
- `slug` (unique) — for SEO-friendly URLs
- `content` — article text (Markdown or HTML)
- `published_at`
- `is_published` — controls public visibility

---

## Fixtures

The app includes pre-loadable fixtures with the project's initial content (philosophy, manifesto).
They are loaded with:

```bash
python manage.py loaddata articles/fixtures/*.json
```

It is recommended to load them on the first deploy and in new development environments.

---

## Endpoints

| Method | URL | Description |
|---|---|---|
| GET | `/api/articles/` | List published articles |
| GET | `/api/articles/<slug>/` | Article detail |
